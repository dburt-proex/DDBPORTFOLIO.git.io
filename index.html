01Validated developer control

DiffWall

A deterministic PR and agent-action firewall for AI-generated changes. DiffWall applies repository-local rules, routes proposed work through ALLOW, REVIEW, or HALT, and preserves evidence before enforcement.

  • Live REVIEW route proven at 48 / 100
  • Live HALT route proven at 100 / 100
  • PR comments and evidence artifacts published before blocking
  • TypeScript PR scanner and Python action validator

Operating method

Architecture that survives contact with execution.

I define the decision model behind a system: what evidence is required, what authority exists, when humans intervene, how failure is contained, and what record remains afterward.

01

Frame the decision

Define the objective, actors, constraints, failure cost, and evidence threshold before choosing tools.

02

Design the control boundary

Specify interfaces, permissions, policy gates, scoring logic, review paths, and state transitions.

03

Instrument the evidence

Capture confidence, unknowns, decisions, exceptions, drift, and rollback information.

04

Ship the operating artifact

Deliver code, schemas, tests, ledgers, SOPs, or evaluation fixtures that make the control model usable.
