Operating method

Systems over spectacle.

I do not stop at a prototype or polished interface. I define the operating model behind the product, how decisions are made, what evidence is required, where humans intervene, and how failure is contained.

01

Frame the decision

Define the objective, actors, constraints, failure cost, and required evidence before selecting tools.

02

Design the control layer

Specify interfaces, policy gates, scoring logic, review paths, and state transitions.

03

Instrument the system

Capture traceability, confidence, unknowns, drift, exceptions, and decision history.

04

Ship the operating artifact

Deliver a usable product, SOP, control spec, evaluation model, or execution workflow.
